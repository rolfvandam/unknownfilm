about unknownfilm.net

Unknownfilm.net came about as an alternative to the iMDB top 250. The iMDB top 250 contains some of the worlds best films but there are only 250 films in it. What if you just like thrillers? What if you're not into old films? What if you've seen absolutely everything and are looking for the most obscure titles?

On unknownfilm.net you just change a few sliders and select which genres, languages and countries to include and scroll down to browse the films that match your criteria.
